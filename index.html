Data Science Projects

Here are some results from my recent research.

Analyzing Avocado Industry

Performed time series analysis using the ARIMA model in Python. Predicted California avocado production and unit prices in 2021 and 2022 using 41 years of historical data.

US Opioid Prescriber Analysis

Explored opioid overdose issues in the United States by analyzing 2019 prescription data in R.

Performed Association Rule Mining to explore relations between prescriber type and opiate drug information.

Prediction of Housing Prices in the US

Performed time series analysis using Prophet in Python to predict monthly single-family residential housing prices in 2022 for each zip code in the US, historical data from 1997 through 2021 were used for the analysis. Choropleth maps were generated to help visualize results.

Maps below present average 1 year and 5 year Return On Investment (ROI) in each state.
